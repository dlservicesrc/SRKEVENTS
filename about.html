About SRK EVENTS

Our Story

SRK EVENTS is not just an event management company; we are dream weavers based in the beautiful city of Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. For over a decade, we've been crafting unforgettable experiences that touch hearts and create lasting memories.

Our journey began 12 years ago with a simple mission: to bring joy and excellence to every service we offer. From our humble beginnings with Mesava Digital Services, Pankaj Office Railway Reservation Counter, and AP RTO Services, we've grown, learned, and evolved.

"Every challenge we faced, every obstacle we overcame, has led us to this moment - where we can now pour our hearts into making your special days truly magical."
